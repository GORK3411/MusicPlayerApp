Smart Music Player (Temporary Name , susseptible to change)

The Smart Music Player is an Android application that allows users to play, organize, and customize their music collection.
Unlike traditional players, it introduces a unique feature: “playlists made of other playlists,” which automatically update when their source playlists change.
The app combines simplicity, automation, and a clean interface to enhance the user’s listening experience.
